# Korean Cucumber Salad

> A light, refreshing cucumber salad with sesame and rice vinegar — perfect alongside Korean beef bowls or any Asian-style dish.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Source** | Social media recipe |
| **Cuisine** | Korean-style |
| **Servings** | 4 |
| **Storage** | Fridge 2 days |

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| English cucumbers, thinly sliced | 2 | 2 |
| Rice vinegar | 1 tbsp | 15ml |
| Sesame oil | 1 tsp | 5ml |
| Sugar | ½ tsp | 2g |
| Salt | pinch | pinch |
| Red pepper flakes (optional) | 1 tsp | 1 tsp |
| Toasted sesame seeds | 1 tsp | 1 tsp |

## Instructions

1. Thinly slice the cucumbers and combine with the vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, salt, pepper flakes, and sesame seeds.
2. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before serving to let the flavors meld.

## Notes

Great as a side for [Korean Beef Bowls](../mains/beef/korean-beef-bowls.md).
