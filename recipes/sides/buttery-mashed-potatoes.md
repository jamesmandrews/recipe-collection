# Buttery Mashed Potatoes

> Silky smooth mashed potatoes loaded with butter and cream, passed through a ricer for the fluffiest texture.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | Classic |
| **Servings** | 6 |
| **Prep Time** | 5 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 20 minutes |
| **Total Time** | 25 minutes |

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Potatoes | 2.2 lb | 1 kg |
| Kosher salt (for cooking) | 2 tsp | 2 tsp |
| Butter | ⅔ cup | 150 g |
| Cream | ¾ cup + 1 tbsp | 200 ml |
| Salt | to taste | to taste |

## Instructions

1. Place the potatoes (skin on) into a pot of cold, well-salted water.
2. Bring to a gentle boil and cook until the potatoes are tender and easily pierced with a knife, about 15–20 minutes depending on size.
3. Drain the potatoes well, then pass them through a sieve or potato ricer for extra fluffiness.
4. While the potatoes are still warm, add the butter and cream and fold into the mash until smooth and fully incorporated.
5. Season with salt to taste and serve.

## Notes

- Use a ricer or sieve for the smoothest results — avoid using a food processor, which can make the potatoes gluey.
- Warm the cream and butter gently before adding for easier incorporation.
