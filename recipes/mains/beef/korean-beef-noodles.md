# Korean Beef Noodles

> Tender slow-cooked beef in a savory gochujang broth, served over noodles and topped with green onions and sesame seeds.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Source** | Social media recipe |
| **Cuisine** | Korean-style |
| **Servings** | 6 |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 6-8 hours (low) or 3-4 hours (high) |

## Ingredients

### Beef and Broth
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Beef chuck, cut into 1-inch pieces | 2 lb | 900g |
| Beef broth | 4 cups | 950ml |
| Water | 2 cups | 475ml |
| Soy sauce | 1 cup | 240ml |
| Brown sugar | ¼ cup | 50g |
| Ginger, minced | 2 tbsp | 12g |
| Garlic, minced | 4 cloves | 4 cloves |
| Sesame oil | 1 tbsp | 15ml |
| Black pepper | 1 tsp | 2g |
| Gochujang (Korean chili paste) | 1 tbsp | 15g |

### Noodles and Garnish
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Fresh or dried noodles (udon or ramen) | 1 lb | 450g |
| Green onions, sliced | 3 | 3 |
| Sesame seeds (optional) | to taste | to taste |
| Fresh cilantro (optional) | to taste | to taste |

## Instructions

1. Season the beef chunks with black pepper.
2. In a bowl, combine beef broth, water, soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, and gochujang. Mix well until the sugar dissolves.
3. Add the seasoned beef to the slow cooker and pour the sauce mixture over the top.
4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours, until the beef is tender.
5. About 15-20 minutes before serving, cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
6. Add the cooked noodles to the slow cooker. Stir gently to combine and allow them to absorb some broth.
7. Scoop the beef and noodle mixture into bowls. Garnish with sliced green onions, sesame seeds, and cilantro if desired.
