# Slow Cooker Mongolian Beef

> Tender thin-sliced beef chuck simmered low and slow in a savory-sweet sauce with fresh ginger and garlic, finished with crisp matchstick carrots. That rich sauce clings to every bite.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | Asian |
| **Servings** | 6 |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 5–6 hours |
| **Total Time** | 5.5–6.5 hours |

## Ingredients

### Beef & Vegetables
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Beef chuck roast, thin-sliced | 2 lb | 900 g |
| Matchstick carrots | 1 cup | 110 g |

### Sauce
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Low-sodium soy sauce | 1 cup | 240 ml |
| Beef broth | 1/2 cup | 120 ml |
| Brown sugar | 1/3 cup | 70 g |
| Garlic, minced | 3 cloves | 3 cloves |
| Fresh ginger, grated | 1 tbsp | 6 g |

### Thickener
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Cornstarch | 2 tbsp | 16 g |
| Water | 2 tbsp | 30 ml |

## Instructions

### Sauce
1. Combine the soy sauce, beef broth, brown sugar, minced garlic, and grated ginger in a bowl and whisk until the sugar dissolves.

### Cook
1. Place the sliced beef in the slow cooker and pour the sauce mixture over top, ensuring the meat is well coated.
2. Cover and cook on LOW for 5–6 hours, or until the beef is very tender.
3. In a small bowl, blend the cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water until smooth. Stir the slurry into the slow cooker sauce.
4. Scatter the matchstick carrots over the beef. Replace the lid and let everything cook for another 20–30 minutes, until the carrots are just tender and the sauce has thickened.
5. Gently toss everything together and serve hot.
