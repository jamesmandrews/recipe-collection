# Thai Basil Beef

> Quick and flavorful stir-fried beef with fresh basil, garlic, and Thai chilis served over jasmine rice with a sunny-side up egg.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Source** | Social media recipe |
| **Cuisine** | Thai-style |
| **Servings** | 2 |

## Ingredients

### Beef Stir-Fry
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Extra lean ground beef | 11 oz | 320g |
| Fresh basil, stems removed | 1 cup | 30g |
| Garlic, finely minced | 3 cloves | 10g |
| Thai red chilis, finely minced | 1-3 | 1-3 |
| Low sodium soy sauce | 2 tbsp | 30ml |
| Raw organic honey | 1 tbsp | 15g |

### To Assemble
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Cooked jasmine rice, divided | 1½ cups | 200g |
| Sunny-side up eggs | 2 | 2 |
| Chili crisp oil (optional) | to taste | to taste |

## Instructions

1. Season and cook ground beef in a greased pan over medium-high heat for ~6 minutes, stirring occasionally before letting it sit slightly to darken/crisp up.
2. Push beef to the side, then add soy sauce, honey, garlic, and chilis. Sauté for ~1 minute, until lightly browned and fragrant.
3. Remove from heat and add basil, stirring until lightly wilted (~1 minute).
4. Serve over jasmine rice and top with a sunny-side up egg. Enjoy!

## Notes

Chili heat level: 1 chili = mild, 2 = medium, 3 = hot.
