# Stovetop Beef Stew

> Hearty stovetop beef stew with thick gravy, tender beef, and real comfort. Chunks of beef simmered low and slow in a rich, savory gravy with tender vegetables. No shortcuts — just a pot on the stove, time, and old-school comfort food that tastes even better the next day.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | American |
| **Servings** | 4–6 |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 1.5–2 hours |
| **Total Time** | 1.75–2.25 hours |

## Ingredients

### Beef
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Beef chuck, cut into chunks | 2.5 lb | 1.1 kg |
| Salt & black pepper | to taste | to taste |

### Base
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Oil or butter | 3 tbsp | 45 ml |
| Large onion, chopped | 1 | 1 |
| Garlic, minced | 3 cloves | 3 cloves |
| All-purpose flour | 3 tbsp | 25 g |

### Liquid
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Beef broth | 4 cups | 960 ml |
| Tomato paste (optional) | 2 tbsp | 30 g |
| Bay leaf | 1 | 1 |
| Worcestershire sauce (optional) | 1 splash | 1 splash |

### Vegetables
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Carrots, sliced | 3 | 3 |
| Celery stalks, sliced | 2 | 2 |
| Potatoes, cubed | 2 | 2 |

## Instructions

### Season & Brown the Beef
1. Season the beef chunks with salt and pepper.
2. Heat oil or butter in a heavy pot over medium-high heat. Brown the beef in batches until well crusted on all sides. Remove and set aside.

### Build the Base
1. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
2. Add the minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds.

### Thicken the Stew
1. Sprinkle the flour over the onions and stir for 1 minute to cook out the raw taste.

### Add Liquid
1. Slowly whisk in the beef broth. Stir in the tomato paste, bay leaf, and Worcestershire sauce if using.

### Slow Simmer
1. Return the browned beef to the pot. Bring to a gentle simmer, cover, and cook for 1–1.5 hours until the beef is tender.

### Add Vegetables
1. Stir in the carrots, celery, and potatoes. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the gravy is thick.

### Serve
1. Remove the bay leaf. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot with bread or biscuits.

## Notes

- For a thicker gravy, simmer uncovered for the last 20 minutes.
- This stew tastes even better the next day.
