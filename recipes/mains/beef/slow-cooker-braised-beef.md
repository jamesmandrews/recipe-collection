# Slow Cooker Braised Beef

> Tender slow cooker braised beef in a rich glossy gravy. This cozy comfort meal melts in your mouth every time.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | American |
| **Servings** | 6 |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 7 hours 15 minutes |
| **Total Time** | 7.5 hours |

## Ingredients

### Beef & Vegetables
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Beef chuck roast, cut into chunks | 2 lb | 900 g |
| Baby potatoes | 1 lb | 450 g |

### Sauce
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Beef broth | 2 cups | 480 ml |
| Soy sauce | 1/4 cup | 60 ml |
| Brown sugar | 2 tbsp | 25 g |
| Garlic, minced | 3 cloves | 3 cloves |
| Worcestershire sauce | 1 tbsp | 15 ml |
| Black pepper | 1 tsp | 2 g |

### Thickener
| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Cornstarch | 1 tbsp | 8 g |
| Water | 2 tbsp | 30 ml |

## Instructions

### Sear
1. Sear beef chunks in a hot pan until browned on all sides.

### Cook
1. Add the seared beef, baby potatoes, minced garlic, and beef broth to the slow cooker.
2. Pour in the soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and brown sugar. Season with black pepper.
3. Cover and cook on LOW for 7 hours, until the beef is fork-tender.
4. In a small bowl, mix the cornstarch and water until smooth to make a slurry. Stir into the slow cooker.
5. Cook for an additional 15 minutes until the sauce has thickened into a glossy gravy.
6. Spoon the sauce over the beef before serving.
