# Pizza Pockets

> Yoghurt dough pizza pockets stuffed with cheese, pepperoni, and marinara. Air fry or bake for a quick high-protein snack.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | American |
| **Servings** | 8 pockets |
| **Air Fryer Temp** | 180°C (356°F) |
| **Oven Temp** | 200°C (392°F) |
| **Calories** | 280 per pocket |

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Low fat Greek yoghurt | 1¾ cups | 410g |
| Plain all purpose flour | 3¼ cups | 400g |
| Baking powder | 2 tsp | 10g |
| Italian herb seasoning | to taste | to taste |
| Garlic powder | to taste | to taste |
| Salt | to taste | to taste |
| Low fat cheese (per pocket) | ¾ oz | 20g |
| Pizza sauce or marinara (per pocket) | 1 tbsp | 15g |
| Sliced mini pepperoni (per pocket) | ¾ oz | 20g |

## Instructions

1. Mix the yoghurt, flour, baking powder, and seasonings until it forms a dough.
2. Cut the dough into 8 pieces and roll each one into a small circle.
3. Spread a little pizza sauce on one half of each circle.
4. Add the cheese and pepperoni.
5. Fold it over and press the edges to seal.
6. Air fry at 180°C for 5-10 minutes, or bake at 200°C for around 5-10 minutes until golden.
7. Let them cool slightly so they don't scorch the roof of your mouth like a lava bomb.
