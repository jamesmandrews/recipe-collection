# Crispy Parmesan Chicken

> A crispy, cheesy chicken that's easy to make. Butterflied chicken breast coated in a garlicky Parmesan egg mixture and pan-fried until golden, served with a fresh side salad.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | Italian-American |
| **Prep Time** | 10 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 15-20 minutes |
| **Total Time** | 25-30 minutes |
| **Servings** | 1 |

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Chicken breast | 5 oz | 150g |
| Flour | 2 tbsp | 30g |
| Eggs | 2 | 2 |
| Salt | 1 tsp | 1 tsp |
| Black pepper | ½ tsp | ½ tsp |
| Garlic, crushed | 3 cloves | 3 cloves |
| Parsley | to taste | to taste |
| Grated Parmesan | 1 oz | 30g |
| Olive oil | 1 tsp | 5ml |
| Side salad | of your choice | of your choice |

## Instructions

1. Slice chicken breast in half and open into a butterfly.
2. On a worktop, using cling film, beat chicken down using a food mallet or rolling pin until 2cm (¾ inch) thick.
3. In a large bowl, add the flour, then add the chicken, coating both sides.
4. In a separate bowl, add eggs and whisk. Then add salt, pepper, garlic, parsley, and Parmesan. Mix with a fork until smooth.
5. Dip the chicken in the egg mixture, coating both sides.
6. Add olive oil to a pan, then fry each side of coated chicken for 7-10 minutes until cooked through.
7. Remove from pan with a spatula and serve with a side salad.
