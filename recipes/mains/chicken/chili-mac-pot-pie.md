# Chili Mac Pot Pie

> Comfort food meets weeknight magic — spicy chicken chili mac topped with a cheesy jalapeño cornbread crust!

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | American |
| **Servings** | 6-8 |

## Ingredients

### Main

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Hot chicken sausage | 1 lb | 450 g |
| Lean ground chicken | 1 lb | 450 g |
| Chili Cheese Hamburger Helper® | 1 box (4.3 oz) | 1 box (122 g) |
| Chicken broth | 2 cups | 480 ml |
| Milk | ⅔ cup | 160 ml |
| Pinto beans, drained | 1 can (15.5 oz) | 1 can (440 g) |
| Fire-roasted tomatoes | 1 can (14.5 oz) | 1 can (411 g) |
| Corn, drained | 1 can (15.25 oz) | 1 can (432 g) |
| Yellow onion, diced | 1 medium | 1 medium |
| Green bell pepper, diced | 1 whole | 1 whole |
| Red bell pepper, diced | 1 whole | 1 whole |
| Jalapeño, diced | 1 whole | 1 whole |
| Sharp cheddar cheese, for topping | ½ cup | 60 g |
| Sour cream (optional) | to taste | to taste |

### Cornbread Topping

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Jiffy® cornbread mix | 1 box | 1 box |
| Buttermilk | ⅓ cup | 80 ml |
| Vegetable oil | 1 tbsp | 15 ml |
| Sharp cheddar cheese, shredded | ⅓ cup | 40 g |
| Jalapeño, diced | 1 whole | 1 whole |

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté onion and peppers 5–7 minutes until soft.
2. Add ground chicken and sausage, cook 7–10 minutes until browned.
3. Stir in pasta, sauce mix, broth, milk, tomatoes, beans, and corn. Simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is al dente.
4. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Pour chili mac into a baking dish and top with cheddar.
5. Mix cornbread with buttermilk, oil, cheese, and jalapeño. Spoon over chili mac.
6. Bake 15–20 minutes until golden. Top with sour cream and extra cheese.
