# Chicken Shawarma Platter

> Juicy yogurt-marinated chicken shawarma with turmeric rice, fresh herb salad, and Greek yogurt tzatziki sauce. Big flavor, easy prep.

## Details

| | |
|-----|-----|
| **Cuisine** | Middle Eastern |
| **Servings** | 4 |
| **Prep Time** | 20 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 30 minutes |
| **Total Time** | 50 minutes |

| Ingredient | Imperial | Metric |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Greek yogurt | 3 tbsp | 45 g |

| Mayonnaise | 2 tbsp | 30 g |

## Instructions

### Turmeric Rice (start first)

1. Rinse rice until the water runs clear. Add rice, water, turmeric, salt, and olive oil to a pot. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer 20–25 minutes. Fluff and set aside.

### Chicken Shawarma

2. Preheat the oven to 425 °F (220 °C).
3. Mix the sliced chicken with olive oil, Greek yogurt, lemon juice and zest, tomato paste, roasted garlic, all the spices, salt, and pepper.
4. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes, stirring once halfway through.

### Fresh Herb Salad

5. Chop the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, and dill. Toss with lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

### Greek Yogurt Tzatziki Sauce

6. Mix all tzatziki ingredients until smooth. Chill until ready to serve.

### Plating

7. Spoon rice onto plates, top with the fresh herb salad, pile on the chicken shawarma, and drizzle with tzatziki sauce.

## Notes

- Approximate macros per plate: 620 cals | 48 g protein | 55 g carbs | 25 g fat.
- Optional: add a fresh pickle on the side.
- Leftover move: stuff everything into naan bread with sliced pickles the next day.
